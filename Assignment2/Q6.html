Delicious Pasta Recipe

Home Recipes Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 100g of grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with sautéed garlic and olive oil.
  5. Add grated cheese and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Each serving contains approximately:

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbs: 60g
  • Fat: 20g
